One-Pan Lemon Herb Chicken and Veggies (Quick Dinner Idea)

This One-Pan meal is an ideal quick dinner idea for busy weeknights. It’s a complete, healthy meal prep solution and a simple family favorite requiring minimal cleanup.

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 35 mins

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts
  • 1.5 lbs small potatoes, quartered (Yukon gold or red)
  • 1 large head of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 large lemon, sliced thinly
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons dried Italian herbs (oregano, basil, thyme)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a large rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper for the easiest cleanup.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the quartered potatoes, broccoli florets, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, Italian herbs, salt, pepper, and minced garlic. Toss until everything is evenly coated.
  3. Spread the vegetable mixture onto the prepared baking sheet in a single layer.
  4. Place the chicken pieces in the same bowl. Add the remaining 2 tablespoons of olive oil and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Toss the chicken to coat.
  5. Arrange the chicken pieces among the vegetables on the baking sheet. Lay the thin lemon slices over the chicken pieces.
  6. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the juices run clear, and the potatoes are tender. For extra crispiness, broil for the last 2-3 minutes.
  7. Serve immediately. This easy recipe makes for a great healthy meal prep for lunches the next day!

Instant Pot 10-Minute Chili Mac

A fast and easy Instant Pot dinner idea that combines classic chili flavors with macaroni pasta. Perfect for a weeknight meal when you need dinner on the table fast.

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins (Pressure)

Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) can chili beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Set the Instant Pot to 'Sauté' mode. Add the ground meat and diced onion. Cook until the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Stir in the tomato sauce, chili beans, beef broth, chili powder, and cumin. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Stir in the dry macaroni. Ensure the pasta is mostly submerged in the liquid.
  4. Cancel 'Sauté' mode. Secure the lid, set the valve to 'Sealing', and pressure cook on 'Manual/High' for 4 minutes.
  5. Once cooking is complete, allow a 5-minute natural pressure release (NPR), then quick release the remaining pressure.
  6. Stir well and let it sit for a couple of minutes to thicken. Serve immediately topped with shredded cheese.

Hearty Classic Meatloaf (Family Favorite)

This is the classic meatloaf recipe your family will ask for again and again. A truly comforting home cooking dish that is easy to prepare and budget friendly.

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 1 hour

Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs or crushed crackers
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • **For the Glaze:** 1/2 cup ketchup, 2 tablespoons brown sugar, 1 tablespoon vinegar.

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Lightly grease a loaf pan or a baking sheet.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the breadcrumbs and milk. Let stand for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the ground beef, egg, chopped onion, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and garlic powder to the bowl. Mix gently with your hands until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Form the mixture into a loaf shape and place it in the prepared pan or on the baking sheet.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the ketchup, brown sugar, and vinegar for the glaze. Spread about half of the glaze evenly over the top of the meatloaf.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove from the oven, spread the remaining glaze on top, and return to the oven for another 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C).
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Simple Budget Ground Beef Tacos

A cheap dinner idea that is big on flavor and easy on the wallet. This simple ground beef recipe is customizable and perfect for feeding a budget friendly family meal.

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Yield: 8 tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet (or 2 tablespoons) taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 8 hard or soft taco shells
  • **Suggested Toppings:** Shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream.

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat. Drain off any excess fat.
  2. Reduce the heat to medium. Stir in the taco seasoning and water.
  3. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 5 to 7 minutes, or until the liquid has mostly reduced and the meat is fully coated in the seasoning.
  4. While the meat simmers, prepare your toppings and warm your taco shells according to the package instructions.
  5. Spoon the seasoned ground beef mixture into the taco shells.
  6. Serve immediately with your favorite toppings. This recipe is also great for making a budget friendly family meal into a taco salad.
