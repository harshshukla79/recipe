Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to Recipes Idea Hub. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. (Last Updated: October 30, 2024)

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information from users:

  • **Personal Information (Directly Provided):** This includes information you voluntarily submit, such as your name and email address when you use the contact form.
  • **Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected):** This includes technical data like your browser type, device type, IP address, and details about how you use the website (pages viewed, time spent, etc.). This information is collected through cookies and tracking technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To analyze website usage trends to improve our marketing and ad targeting efforts.
  • For compliance with legal obligations and to protect against fraud or abuse.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. A cookie is a small file stored on your device that tracks usage data. We use functional cookies (to remember your preferences, like dismissing the cookie banner) and analytics/advertising cookies (used by partners like Google Ads to display relevant advertisements).

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of these cookies as described in this policy and our cookie banner.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal contact information. We may share non-personal, aggregated, or anonymized data with third parties for business analysis, advertising (Google Ads), or other purposes.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to other websites (e.g., links to food suppliers or kitchen equipment). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any site you visit outside of Recipes Idea Hub.

6. Your Privacy Choices

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. However, this may affect your ability to use certain features of the website. If you wish to opt out of personalized advertising, you should consult your browser's documentation.
